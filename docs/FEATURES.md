# 🌟 Features: Egyptian Omnichannel Community Growth Specialist

The most advanced, cost-optimized marketing automation system tailored specifically for the Egyptian digital landscape. Built on **CrewAI** and powered by a multi-provider **Omni-Model Architecture**.

---

## 🇪🇬 Egyptian Market Localization
Unlike generic marketing tools, this system is "born and raised" in Egypt:
- **Amiya Masriya (Egyptian Arabic)**: Agents are prompted to use authentic local dialects, street slang, and cultural hooks to maximize engagement.
- **Governorate Intelligence**: Content is adapted based on local nuances (e.g., Cairo vs. Alexandria vs. Upper Egypt).
- **Cultural Calendar**: Awareness of Egyptian seasonal trends, religious events (Ramadan, Eids), and national holidays.

---

## 🧠 Omni-Model "Thinking" Layer
Optimized for both performance and cost-efficiency:
- **Multi-Provider Support**: Switch between **Kimi (Moonshot)**, **OpenAI**, **Anthropic**, **Gemini**, and **Mistral**.
- **Agent-Specific LLMs**: Use cheap, fast models (Kimi) for web discovery and complex, high-reasoning models (Gemini Pro) for creative strategy.
- **Dynamic Routing**: Configurable via a user-friendly GUI—no coding required.

---

## 🎨 Multimodal Media Generation
Autonomous creation of high-end visual assets:
- **Images**: Integrated with **Google Gemini Imagen**, **DALL-E 3**, and **Stability AI**.
- **Videos**: Supports **Google Gemini Veo**, **Runway (Gen-2/3)**, and **Luma AI** for cinematic production-quality video.
- **Brand Consistency**: Media Director agent analyzes the content draft to ensure visuals match the Egyptian audience's aesthetic preferences.

---

## 🔍 Unlimited Community Discovery
Internet-wide research without the cost of paid APIs:
- **SearXNG Integration**: A self-hosted, private search engine that allows for unlimited queries across the entire web.
- **Platform Coverage**: Discovers Egyptian Facebook Groups, WhatsApp Communities, Telegram Channels, and niche local forums.
- **Scoring System**: Automatically evaluates communities based on audience size, engagement quality, and moderation strictness.

---

## 🔌 ERPNext Integration
Direct bridge to your business data:
- **Campaign Sync**: Pulls marketing objectives and budgets directly from ERPNext.
- **Product Awareness**: Real-time access to item descriptions, variants, pricing (EGP), and inventory levels.
- **Lead Generation**: (Roadmap) Capabilities to push engagement leads back into the ERPNext CRM.

---

## 🖥️ Dual-Interface Experience
Graphical control for every type of user:
- **Streamlit Admin Dashboard**: 
    - Manage the "Human-in-the-Loop" workflow.
    - Review and Approve/Reject Egyptian Arabic posts.
    - Visually configure which AI model each agent uses.
- **CrewUI**: 
    - Real-time visualization of agent "thought processes."
    - Detailed logs of how agents collaborate and use tools.

---

## 🛡️ Security & State Management
- **Standalone DB**: Uses a dedicated SQL database (SQLite/PostgreSQL) to store all transient AI data, keeping your ERP clean.
- **Manual/Assisted Approval**: Full control over what gets published. Nothing goes live without human consent (configurable).
- **Dockerized Architecture**: One-click deployment for all services (Search, API, Dashboard, UI).
